About lalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalalala.com (26× 'la')

This page is an intentionally absurd internet meme: a fullscreen repeating animation and a looping "lalalalala" soundtrack meant to be silly and shareable. The domain uses a playful repetition of "la" to emphasize the joke.

Origin / Context

For context the meme is documented on Desciclopedia — a satirical wiki that catalogs humorous internet phenomena. This site is a light-hearted tribute to that kind of absurd humor.
